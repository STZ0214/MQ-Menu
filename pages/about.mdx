# About MASTER Q

## Our Story

Established in 2010, Master Q combines professional billard sports with a premium bar experience. What started as a passion project between friends has grown into...

- 🎱 18 professional-grade billard tables
- 🍸 Craft cocktail bar with 20+ spirits
- 📺 9 HD screens for sports viewing
- 🎉 Private event space for up to 100 people
